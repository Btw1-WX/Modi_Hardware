What we do


We as a company specialize in providing a wide range of high-quality building materials. Our product portfolio includes shuttering ply, which is essential for constructing sturdy formwork structures. We also offer flash doors, WPC boards, single and double core boards of different thicknesses, 18mm ply, laminates (Sunmica), clades, MDF, teak ply, and veneers. Whether you're working on a construction project or looking to enhance your interiors, our diverse product lineup ensures that you have access to durable, versatile, and aesthetically pleasing materials.
